About SentiraX

Last updated: February 8, 2026

SentiraX is a mood-aware movie and TV discovery companion. It blends your taste, your current mood, and AI ranking to help you quickly decide what to watch next.

SentiraX does not host or stream any video content. We only provide discovery, metadata, and links to external platforms.

What SentiraX Does

  • Builds a lightweight taste profile (genres, goals, preferences)
  • Lets you check in with your current mood and vibe
  • Generates AI-ranked movie and show suggestions
  • Shows detailed cast and availability information
  • Supports a personal watchlist stored in your browser
  • Offers direct title search powered by TMDB

How It Works

  1. Baseline setup: You set your age and optional taste preferences.
  2. Mood check-in: You share how you feel and what you want to watch.
  3. AI picks: SentiraX uses AI to suggest real titles and enriches them with TMDB data.
  4. Details: You can view cast, providers, and Wikipedia summaries.

Direct Search

If you already have a title in mind, the Search page lets you look up movies or shows directly from TMDB, then open full details instantly.

Data & Privacy

  • Your profile, watchlist, and settings are stored locally in your browser.
  • Recommendation requests are sent to the SentiraX backend to generate results.
  • We do not collect personal identifiers like your name or email.

Data Sources & Credits

  • TMDB (The Movie Database) for posters, metadata, and search
  • Wikipedia for extended descriptions and background
  • LLM providers for AI-based ranking and summaries

This product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB.
