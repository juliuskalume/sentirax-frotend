Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9th December, 2025

1. Introduction

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information SentiraX collects and how we use it. By using SentiraX, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. No Personal Information Collected

We do not collect your name, email, address, phone number, or any personally identifying information.

2.2. Anonymous Usage Data

We may collect anonymous technical data such as:

  • Browser and device type
  • Pages visited
  • Session duration
  • Error logs

2.3. Local Storage (Stored on Your Device)

Your watchlist, filters, and preferences are stored locally in your browser. This data never leaves your device and is never sent to our servers.

2.4. Cookies (Advertising)

SentiraX uses third-party advertising services such as Google AdSense. These services may use cookies to:

  • Personalize ads
  • Measure ad performance
  • Prevent showing the same ad repeatedly

You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

3. Third-Party Services

SentiraX interacts with several external services:

3.1. TMDB API

Used for movie/TV metadata such as posters and descriptions.

This product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB.

3.2. Wikipedia API

Used to fetch additional informational content.

3.3. Groq / LLM Providers

Used to generate AI-based recommendations.

3.4. Google AdSense

Used to display advertisements. Their own Privacy Policy and cookie practices apply.

4. External Links

Clicking links to Netflix, Prime Video, or other platforms sends you to third-party websites. Their privacy policies apply and we do not control them.

5. Data Security

We do not store sensitive user data. Local storage is controlled entirely by your browser.

6. Children’s Privacy

SentiraX is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.
